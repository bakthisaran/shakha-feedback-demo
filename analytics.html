Score heatmap — session × gana
Full picture at a glance. Colour = avg score (red → amber → green)
What you're looking at: Every session (columns) crossed with every gana (rows). Each cell shows the average rating that gana gave that session, colour-coded from red (low) to green (high).

What to look for:
  • A whole column is red — that session scored poorly across all ganas.
  • A whole row is red — one gana rated everything low.
  • Mixed colours in a column — ganas disagreed on that session.
  • Bright green cells — the strongest sessions and ganas.
Gana ranking
Overall avg across all sessions
What you're looking at: Each gana's average rating collapsed across every session and every date in the current filter.

What to look for:
  • Large gap between top and bottom — some ganas are systematically more positive or critical.
  • All ganas clustered closely — broad consensus on quality.
Gana agreement
How closely ganas agree per session (lower gap = more consensus)
What you're looking at: For each session, the spread is the gap between the highest and lowest gana average.

Colour guide:
  • Green (below 0.5) — strong consensus.
  • Amber (0.5–1.0) — moderate disagreement.
  • Red (above 1.0) — significant disagreement.
